###

This is a scanner that will work based off scanning a barcode using a flask website that will call data from a SQLite Database


What needs to be done includes:

Create a 15 barcode randomly generated based on the price and name of the object

Append the barcode to the SQLite database that will also include: Name, Picture, Price of the item

Flask app will host the app and will allow you to scan the product:
   -You will get a picture
   -Price of the object
   -Object Name

   Bonus features:

   You will also be able to create new products that are locked on based on your store number
